# How to Apply for PAN Card Online in 2026

Applying for a PAN card is one of the most important steps for every Indian citizen today. Whether you want to open a bank account, file income tax returns, invest in mutual funds, or start a business, having a PAN card is mandatory.

Earlier, applying for a PAN card required visiting government offices and submitting physical forms. But now the process has become much easier because you can apply for a PAN card online from the comfort of your home.

This guide explains the complete step-by-step process to apply for a PAN card online in India, including documents required, fees, processing time, and common mistakes to avoid.

---

## üìå What is a PAN Card?

PAN stands for **Permanent Account Number**, which is issued by the Income Tax Department of India. It is a unique 10-digit alphanumeric identity used for tracking financial transactions.

A PAN card is required for:

* Filing income tax returns
* Opening a bank account
* Buying property or vehicles
* Investing in stocks or mutual funds
* Applying for loans

Without a PAN card, many financial activities cannot be completed.
